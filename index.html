Battery Arbitrage

Under very simple assumptions, how long would it take to pay off a grid-connected battery doing nothing more than charging when cheap and discharging when expensive?

A New Chart of History

Reproducing one of my favourite data visualisations, Joseph Priestley's New Chart of History, as a modern web app, minus the Classicist bias towards the Roman Empire.

Global Trade Networks

For a wide array of product categories, visualise the bilateral trade flows around the world between the countries that are the biggest exporters and importers.

Poetic Metre Parser

Tool for evaluating the metre (stress structure) of a given text and guessing whether it follows a given metric structure, e.g. iambic pentameter.
